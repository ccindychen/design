Zodiac Kiosk

Context: The MTA installed interactive kiosks on train platforms so riders can look up service information while waiting for their trains. The screens also show ads and public service announcements every few minutes. For this assignment, I had to design a kiosk that will capture the attention of subway riders and create an intuitive, easy-to-use interface for them to look up their horoscopes.


I have one minute to sell the idea to riders and get them to tap. Once they tap, they can play with your interface as long as they like.

The animal icons and style of the kiosk was initially designed on Adobe InDesign as a poster


A visually attractive intro screen to catch the attention of subway goers.

Informs the user of their chinese zodiac sign and a description of personalities assoicated to people with that sign

Prototype of the interaction designed using figma
